About CivicAlert

A platform built to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower citizens to report civic issues and hold local authorities accountable for timely resolution of community problems.

👥

Who can use it

Any citizen can register and submit complaints. Local authorities manage and resolve them. Admins oversee the entire system.

🔒

Privacy & Security

Your data is protected with JWT authentication and encrypted passwords. We never share your personal information.

📱

How to get started

Register a free account, submit your first complaint with a photo and location, and track its progress in real time.

Built with

Express.js Node.js MongoDB JWT Auth Leaflet.js Cloudinary Nodemailer OpenStreetMap

Contact

For issues or suggestions, submit a complaint or reach out at support@civicalert.in
